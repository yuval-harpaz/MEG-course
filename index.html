MEG-course

MEG course at Bar Ilan University, Israel. using mainly FieldTrip and SAM beamforming.

Our Magnetoencephalograph is 4D-Neuroimaging with 248 magnetometers. the course contains matlab scripts for analyzing MEG data. SAM beamforming is not matlab and the software is not free (obtained from NIMH). dependencies: ft_BIU cleanMEG_BIU SAM_BIU vsMovies AFNI matlab pack work-drafts

course1 Reading the data with pdf4D

course2 Methods for viewing the data, filters and baseline correction

course3 CreateCleanFile function, the trigger channel

course4 Component analysis and visual rejection

course5 Frequency and Time Frequency analysis

course6 Dipole fit for epilepsy and auditory data

course7 SAM beamforming

course8 Beamforming with FieldTrip

course9 beamforming with FieldTrip 2

course10 Connectivity

course11 Sensor level statistics

course12 Source level statistics

course12 Permutation test for dependent samples ttest with correction for multiple comparisons
